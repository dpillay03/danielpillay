<center><h1 className="blog-header">If You Don't Jump, You Will Never Fly</h1></center>
<br>
<p className="blog-content">Nothing in life has been more exhilarating, terrifying, and gratifying than skydiving. In July of 2018, my mom approached me and asked if we could go skydiving for her birthday. I wasn’t shocked because if you know my mom personally, this is right up her alley. I agreed and before I knew it, I was plummeting to the earth from 12,000 feet in the air. As soon as I got home that day, I was still shaking off the post-jump high and I began to think about how exciting it would be to be able to jump on my own. I made the appointment to attend AFF (Accelerated Free Fall) school at a drop zone in Washington State. Although I have had 9 jumps so far, this is the only time I asked to be recorded. During your first jump, two instructors are there to stabalize you. Once the parachute is deployed, the student is responsible for piloting his chute towards a successful landing.</p>
